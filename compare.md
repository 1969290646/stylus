
## Implementation Comparisons

Below we go head to head with other implementations.

### Variables

Webkit:

     @var main-color color #006
     color: var(main-color);

SCSS:

     $main-color: #006;
     color: $main-color;

Less:

     @main-color: #006;
     color: @main-color;

Stylus:

     main-color = #006
     color main-color
